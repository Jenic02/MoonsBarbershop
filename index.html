About Us

Where Craftsmanship Meets Style

At Creative Edge, every cut is a statement. We blend traditional barbering techniques with modern styles to give you a look that turns heads.

Creative Edge is your go-to barbershop in Silverdale. Mike, our owner and master barber, has been serving the community with precision haircuts, beard trims, and great conversation. He remembers every client and makes sure you leave looking your best.

Whether you need a clean fade, a classic trim, or just want to catch up, Creative Edge is the place. Walk in, sit down, and let us take care of the rest.
