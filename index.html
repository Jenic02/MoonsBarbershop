About Us

Where Craftsmanship Meets Style

At Moon's Barber, every cut is a statement. We blend traditional barbering techniques with modern styles to give you a look that turns heads.

Moon's Barber has been a cornerstone of the Silverdale community, known for precision haircuts, relaxing hot towel shaves, and the kind of personal service that keeps clients coming back for years.

Our barbers are masters of their craft, trained in both classic and contemporary techniques. Whether you need a sharp fade, a classic pompadour, or a clean straight-razor shave, we deliver every time.
